I studied painting at the Glasgow School of Art. I keep a studio in SE Industrial Portland, which is for rent when I travel.

I founded this Research Club but definitely not this one. I would love to talk to you about alternative education projects.

I produce websites and projects with the brilliant delinquents at Bletchley Park. I'm available to freelance.

Before I did any of that, I worked in rare books for 7 years with some wonderful people

I've got a heavy case of synesthesia and am working on a synesthesia starter kit with the good people of r/Synesthesia. Contributors welcome.

I love Tai Chi and practice Sun and Chen Man Ching style, often with the talented and patient folks who meet in Laurlhurst Park in Portland, OR.
